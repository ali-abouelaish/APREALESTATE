Our Story

Founded in 2009, AP Real Estate Solutions was born from a simple vision: to revolutionize property management by putting people first. What started as a small team managing a handful of properties has grown into one of London's most trusted property management companies.

Our founder, recognizing the gaps in traditional property management, set out to create a service that truly served both landlords and tenants. By focusing on guaranteed rent, proactive maintenance, and transparent communication, we've built a reputation for excellence that speaks for itself.

Today, we manage over 500 properties across London, employing a dedicated team of professionals who share our commitment to exceptional service. But despite our growth, we've never lost sight of our core values: integrity, reliability, and genuine care for our clients.
