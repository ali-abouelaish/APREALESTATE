Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about selling your property

Property values depend on many factors including location, size, condition, and current market conditions. Our experienced valuers will provide a free, no-obligation market appraisal based on recent comparable sales and local market knowledge. Book your free valuation today.

Our commission starts from 1.5% + VAT, with no upfront fees. You only pay when your property successfully sells - no sale, no fee. The exact rate depends on your property value and chosen package. Contact us for a detailed quote tailored to your property.

On average, our properties sell within 8 weeks from listing to completion. However, this varies based on property type, price, location, and market conditions. Properties priced competitively with excellent marketing typically attract offers within 2-4 weeks.

Yes, an EPC is legally required before marketing your property. It must be valid (less than 10 years old) and available to potential buyers. If you don't have one, we can arrange this for you through our network of certified assessors at a competitive rate.
